Items capture what was sold, in what quantity, and at which price. Edits flow into totals, shipments, and status tracking.

![Order detail items and totals](/screenshots/open-mercato-orders-order-details2.png)

## Manage line items

- Open the **Items** tab to add products, set quantities, and adjust pricing.
- Line-level statuses (from **Order line statuses**) track fulfillment stages such as pending, picking, packed, shipped, delivered, returned.
- Item edits recalc subtotals and downstream adjustments automatically.

![Order line statuses dictionary](/screenshots/open-mercato-orders-settings-order-line-statuses.png)

## Best practices

- Keep line statuses aligned with your warehouse states so shipment updates stay clear.
- Use discounts via **Adjustments** rather than manual price edits to preserve margin visibility.
