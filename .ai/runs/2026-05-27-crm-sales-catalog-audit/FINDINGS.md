## How "fix in this PR" was chosen

S-01 is the single critical-severity finding. It is a confirmed cross-tenant write surface (a payment allocation can attach to another tenant's order or invoice), the fix mirrors the pattern already in use a few lines above on line 625-629 (`findOneWithDecryption` + `ensureSameScope`), and it can be covered by focused unit tests without touching the wider sales flow. The remaining findings either require larger refactors (S-04, K-04: cross-module decoupling), are owned by separate code paths that warrant their own PRs (S-02, S-03, S-05), or are mediums/lows better tackled in dedicated tickets.
