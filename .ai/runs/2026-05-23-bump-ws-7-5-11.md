## Goal

Re-target Dependabot PR [#2031](https://github.com/open-mercato/open-mercato/pull/2031) (`chore(deps): bump ws from 7.5.10 to 7.5.11`) from `main` to `develop`, so the next release cycle consumes the backported `ws@7.5.11` security fix without leaving a stale Dependabot branch against `main`. After the develop-targeting PR is opened, close the original #2031.

## Scope

`ws@7.5.11` is a maintenance release on the `7.x` line that backports a single security/buffer fix (`e14c4586` — "Limit retained message parts"). The diff is a one-line bump in `yarn.lock` against the `ws@npm:^7.3.1` resolution slot.

### In scope (apply)

| Package | Range on develop | From | To | File |
|---------|-----------------|------|----|------|
| `ws` (transitive, `^7.3.1` slot) | `^7.3.1` | `7.5.10` | `7.5.11` | `yarn.lock` |

Verified on `origin/develop`: `ws@npm:^7.3.1` currently resolves to `7.5.10`. The other two ws ranges on develop (`^8.17.1`/`^8.18.0` and `~8.18.3`) are unaffected by the 7.x backport and stay put.

### Out of scope

- Direct `package.json` edits — `ws` is purely transitive at the `^7.3.1` constraint; only `yarn.lock` changes.
- Any change to `ws` 8.x consumers.

### Phase 1: Apply the ws bump on a develop-based branch
